Day One Reflections

I have the impression that all of these things are words I have heard before, and ideas I have encountered, but just barely, like light brushes with concepts with no previous need for deep understanding.

I feel also like there are a lot of things I learned in the prework I did manage to do, but I only learned it in a sort of superficial way because I'm not really able to reproduce it without looking up every single thing. I also feel the need to get my work checked. I am not sure if this is good practice or just a natural desire for validation. Will monitor going forward.

Emily Lemmon

Day Two Reflections

My initial feelings of being swamped are a bit mitigated. There is a lot of surface-scratching, inevitable but difficult to feel satisfied with, but I'm just tuning in the way I actually tend to do best. Instead of frantically trying to cover everything and have all the details in order, I should try to just get the concept, because the notes are availalbe and if I can get to where I can figure out the path back to them, I'll be fine.
